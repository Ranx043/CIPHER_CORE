```

│
├── 1. IDEACIÓN (1-2 meses)

│   ├── Solution hypothesis

│   ├── Competitive analysis
│   └── Initial team formation

├── 2. VALIDACIÓN (2-3 meses)
│   ├── Technical feasibility
│   ├── Economic model draft
│   ├── Community validation
│   ├── Advisor recruitment

│   ├── Testnet deployment
│   ├── Initial audits
│   ├── Documentation
│   └── Community building starts
│
├── 4. LANZAMIENTO (1-2 meses)
│   ├── Mainnet deployment
│   ├── Token launch (if applicable)
│   ├── Liquidity provision
│   ├── Marketing push
│   └── Exchange listings
│
├── 5. CRECIMIENTO (6-12+ meses)
│   ├── User acquisition
│   ├── Feature expansion
│   ├── Partnership development
│   ├── Protocol upgrades
```

```yaml
token_design_framework:

  utilidad:
    preguntas_clave:
      - ¿Para qué sirve el token?
      - ¿Por qué alguien lo querría?
      - ¿Se puede lograr lo mismo sin token?

    tipos_utilidad:
      governance:
        - Votación en propuestas
        - Control de parámetros
        - Treasury decisions

      access:
        - Premium features
        - Priority access
        - Gated communities

      fees:
        - Fee discounts
        - Fee sharing
        - Revenue rights

      staking:
        - Security staking
        - Liquidity mining
        - Yield generation

      payment:
        - In-protocol payments
        - Service access
        - Gas token

  supply_design:
    considerations:
      - Total supply (fixed vs inflationary)
      - Initial circulating supply
      - Inflation/deflation mechanics
      - Burn mechanisms

    common_patterns:
      fixed_supply:
        example: 100,000,000 tokens
        pros: Scarcity narrative
        cons: Less flexibility

      capped_inflation:
        example: 2% annual max
        pros: Sustainable rewards
        cons: Dilution concern

      deflationary:
        example: Fee burns
        pros: Positive tokenomics
        cons: Requires volume
```

### 5.1 Consideraciones Legales

```yaml
legal_framework:

  entity_structure:
    common_patterns:
      foundation:

      dao:
        type: Varies by jurisdiction
        purpose: Governance, treasury

    multi_entity:
      pattern: Foundation + Labs + DAO
      example:
        - Uniswap Foundation (grants)
        - Uniswap Labs (development)
        - Uniswap DAO (governance)

  token_classification:
    utility_token:
      definition: Access/use of protocol
      considerations:
        - No expectation of profit
        - Functional at launch
        - Decentralized

    security_token:
      definition: Investment contract (Howey Test)
      triggers:
        - Promise of returns
        - Centralized development
        - No utility at launch

    regulatory_risk:
      - SEC (US) very aggressive
      - MiCA (EU) new framework
      - Varies by jurisdiction

  jurisdictions:
    crypto_friendly:
      - Switzerland (Crypto Valley)
      - Singapore
      - Dubai/UAE
      - Cayman Islands
      - British Virgin Islands
      - Portugal (personal)

      - United States
      - China
      - India (unclear)

    - Terms of Service
```
